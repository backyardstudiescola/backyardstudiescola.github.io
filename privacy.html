🏆 TrophyLife — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

1. Introduction

TrophyLife ("we," "our," or "us") is operated by Backyard Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application TrophyLife.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Email address and password when you create an account
  • Custom habit names and categories you create

Information collected automatically:

  • Habit completion data and streak information
  • App usage data and session information
  • Device type, operating system version, and device identifier

Information collected by third parties:

  • Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve advertisements. See Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the TrophyLife service
  • To track your habit completion and calculate your virtual income
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To prevent fraud and ensure one account per device
  • To improve our app and fix bugs

4. Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely using Supabase. Your habit progress and balance are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Virtual Currency and Rewards

TrophyLife uses a virtual income system. Trophy tokens and cash balance figures are virtual and have no real monetary value until redemption thresholds are met and verified by Backyard Studios. Redemptions are subject to availability and verification. Backyard Studios reserves the right to verify user accounts before processing any redemption request.

6. Advertising

TrophyLife uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use cookies and device identifiers to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings under Privacy → Advertising.

7. Age Restriction

TrophyLife is intended for users aged 17 and older. By using this app you confirm that you are at least 17 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 17. If we discover that a user is under 17 we will delete their account immediately.

8. Device Identification

To prevent fraud and ensure fair use of the rewards system, TrophyLife collects a unique device identifier and associates it with your account. Only one TrophyLife account is permitted per device.

9. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
